Shake Flashlight — Privacy Policy

Effective date: October 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Shake Flashlight ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and your choices. By installing or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve the App. The categories below describe the types of information we may collect.

2.1 Information you provide

  • Contact information: If you contact support or send feedback, we may collect your email address and any information you provide.
  • Optional settings: Any settings you enter or preferences you save inside the App.

2.2 Automatically collected information

  • Device identifiers: Advertising ID, device model, OS version, unique app instance identifiers (to operate and update the App).
  • Usage data: App open/close events, feature usage, crash reports, and analytics to help us understand how the App is used and to fix bugs.
  • Sensor data: Motion/accelerometer data processed on-device to detect the shake gesture used to trigger the flashlight. We do not record raw continuous sensor streams or store precise motion traces on our servers by default.
  • Log data: Timestamps and minor diagnostic data collected when the App crashes or encounters errors.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's core functionality (e.g., enabling the flashlight when a shake is detected).
  • To analyze and improve features, performance, and stability.
  • To communicate with you when you contact support or request updates.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the rights, property or safety of the App, its users, or others.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share information in the following limited situations:

  • Service providers: We use third-party services (for crash reporting, analytics, cloud hosting) that process limited technical and usage data on our behalf. These providers are contractually restricted from using data for other purposes.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, sale, or asset transfer, user data may be transferred as part of that transaction under similar privacy protections.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Analytics and Advertising

We may use analytics providers to collect aggregate, non-identifying usage information to help improve the app. If we show ads via third-party ad networks, those networks may collect data as described in their privacy policies. You can opt out of personalized advertising at the device level through your platform settings.

6. Permissions

The App requests only the permissions necessary for its functionality. Typical permissions include:

  • Camera / Flash access: Required to operate the flashlight. The app does not take pictures or record video.
  • Motion / Sensors: Access to accelerometer/gyroscope for shake detection. Sensor processing is performed on-device.
  • Optional: Network access to fetch updates or send crash reports.

You can revoke permissions in your device settings; however, revoking camera/flash access will disable the flashlight feature.

7. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business needs (e.g., debugging, analytics). If you request deletion of your data, we will remove personally identifying information from our systems, subject to any legal retention requirements.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information, contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the data we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Correction: You may request access to or correction of personal information you provided by contacting us.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of analytics and marketing communications by following the instructions in those messages or by contacting us.
  • Delete: You may request deletion of your personal data. We will comply unless we are required to retain certain information by law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If material changes occur, we will notify you by posting the new policy in the App and updating the "Effective date" above. Your continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
